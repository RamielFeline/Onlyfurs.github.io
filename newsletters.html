{What I have been up to} 23/6/2023

Starting with what I have been doing as of late. I am looking to change career to something I actually want to do for once. I figure I can start here at the very least. Becoming a full stack web dev is at least a start, I could at least try to aim for being a platform developer after I get started on that. I am re-learning what I already know of HTML, CSS, JS, through free online cources and then moving onto the rest for backend work, PHP comes to mind but it's very late and I haven't slept for real in about 40 hours. The fact I can even type is a miracle in of itself.

As much as I would like to say I have been super happy landing the job I have I have been looking more into myself these days, turns out my original thought of what I should do was correct, persue code and programming, thats what I should have actually tried to do instead of giving up on such a dream forever ago. Oh well, things happen I guess, live and learn.

Along the way I have made the most amazing friends I could ever ask for, friends who are supportive, knowledgeable and caring. How I ended up here I don't know other than it took me finally breaking out of my isolative habits and striking up convversations that cultivate into full blown relationships with those amazing people.

If there is one thing I could think of for what I could pass on to the future generations; You have dreams and desires for a reason. If you are not good at those things right now, persue them anyways and practice, keep trying, don't give up, and always treat the opportunity to learn as a gift. Swallow your damn pride.
